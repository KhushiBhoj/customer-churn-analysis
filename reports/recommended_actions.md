# Recommended Actions Based on Churn Risk

This section translates model predictions into actionable business strategies.
Customers are segmented based on predicted churn risk, enabling targeted
retention and value-optimization efforts.

---

## Churn Risk Segments & Actions

| Risk Segment | Customer Profile | Key Signals | Recommended Actions | Business Impact |
|-------------|------------------|-------------|----------------------|-----------------|
| **High Risk** | Short tenure, month-to-month contracts, high monthly charges | High churn probability, price sensitivity | Immediate retention offers, contract upgrades, proactive support | Reduced short-term churn |
| **Medium Risk** | Moderate tenure, mixed contracts | Emerging churn signals | Loyalty campaigns, service bundling, feedback surveys | Prevent churn escalation |
| **Low Risk** | Long tenure, long-term contracts | Stable behavior | Loyalty rewards, referrals, upselling | Increased lifetime value |
| **Very Low Risk** | Long-term, multi-service customers | Strong retention signals | Minimal intervention | Cost-efficient retention |

---

## Key Insights
- Month-to-month contracts and higher-than-average charges are the strongest churn drivers.
- Early intervention in medium-risk customers can significantly reduce future churn.
- Targeted actions outperform blanket retention strategies.

---

## Business Value
Risk-based segmentation allows marketing and customer success teams to:
- Prioritize high-impact customers
- Reduce unnecessary incentives
- Align retention strategy with predictive analytics
